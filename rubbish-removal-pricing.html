What affects pricing

Volume: The main factor. We measure in cubic metres or trailer loads.

Weight: Heavy items like concrete or soil cost more to dispose of.

Access: Stairs, long carries, or difficult access may increase time.

Waste type: Special disposal requirements for certain materials.

All factors are included in your upfront quote. No surprises on the day.

What is included

All quotes include: labour for loading and carrying, transport to disposal, all disposal and recycling fees, cleanup of the area, and public liability insurance coverage.

One fixed price. We do not add fees during the job unless scope changes significantly.

What we do not remove

Hazardous: Asbestos, chemicals, paint, solvents, gas bottles, batteries, medical waste.

Restricted: Firearms, ammunition, explosives.

Contact us if unsure. We can suggest appropriate disposal for restricted items.
