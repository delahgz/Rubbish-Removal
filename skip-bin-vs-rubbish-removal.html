When rubbish removal is better

Choose a rubbish removal service when you want convenience over cost savings. Our service is ideal when you live in an apartment or unit with no driveway, have heavy items like fridges or concrete, need items removed from inside your home, want same day service, or simply do not want to do the physical work.

The total cost is often similar to skip bin hire once you factor in your time, the risk of injury, and potential permit fees.

When a skip bin makes sense

Skip bins can be cost effective for large ongoing projects where you are generating waste over several days, like major renovations. They work well if you have easy driveway access, do not mind doing the loading yourself, and have time to fill the bin over multiple days.

Be aware of hidden costs: weight-based surcharges, permit fees for street placement, and extension fees if you need it longer than expected.

Cost comparison

For a typical garage cleanout (4-6 cubic metres), expect to pay $300-500 for professional removal including all labour and disposal. A 4m³ skip bin costs $300-400 for 3-7 days, but you do all the loading. Add $50-100 for council permits if needed, plus potential weight surcharges.

When you value your time at even $30/hour, the 4-6 hours of loading a skip bin can eliminate any savings. For single items or smaller loads, removal service is almost always more cost effective.
